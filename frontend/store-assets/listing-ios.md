## Description (4000 characters max)

```
Sakina is a quiet space for mindful wellness. It gives you a place to notice
how you're feeling, think it through with a supportive AI companion, and stay
connected to a small community that's doing the same work.

What you can do with Sakina:

• Keep a private journal with mood, emotions, and energy tracking. See the
  patterns that emerge over weeks without having to do the math.

• Talk it through. Our AI companion is trained to listen and reflect, not
  diagnose. It remembers your recent sessions so conversations actually
  continue.

• Join small, moderated community groups focused on things like sleep, study
  stress, or just general check-ins. You choose what to share, and you can
  block or report anything that feels off.

• Receive a daily wellness tip, picked from a curated library of 160+ prompts
  across sleep, nutrition, mindfulness, study, movement, organization, and
  social connection.

• Export your data at any time, delete your account from inside the app, and
  control every notification preference from a clear settings screen.

Sakina is not a medical device and does not replace professional care. If
you're in crisis, please reach out to a local support line or emergency
services — we list them from inside the Help & Support screen.

Privacy first:
• Your journal entries and chat messages are encrypted at rest on our servers.
• We don't sell your data. We don't track you across other apps or websites.
• You can read our full privacy policy before creating an account.

Free to use for the core features. No ads. No affiliate links.
```

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 18, 2026 · Last updated: April 18, 2026

Sakina (“Sakina”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a mobile application that provides AI-assisted mental wellness support, mood journaling, guided reflection, and a moderated peer community. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and the rights you have over that information. By creating a Sakina account or using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Sakina is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a substitute for professional care. If you are in crisis or think you may harm yourself or others, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account information: username, email address, and a salted BCrypt hash of your password.
  • Optional contact information: phone number if you choose to enable SMS-based two-factor verification.
  • Journal content: mood logs, emotions, energy levels, free-text journal entries, and timestamps.
  • Chat content: messages you send to the AI wellness assistant and the assistant’s replies.
  • Community content: posts, replies, reactions, reports, and the pseudonymous display name shown to other users.
  • Assessments: responses to optional self-assessments (e.g., PHQ-9, GAD-7) and derived scores.
  • Notification preferences: the channels you enable and the delivery times you choose.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and session data: device type, operating system version, app version, language, and time zone.
  • Authentication events: login attempts, login timestamps, and source IP address, retained for abuse prevention.
  • Diagnostic data: crash reports and error telemetry, used solely to fix defects.
  • Push tokens: anonymous device tokens issued by Apple APNs or Google Firebase so we can deliver reminders you have opted into.

1.3 Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not access your contacts, microphone, or camera.
  • We do not purchase personal data from data brokers.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the core features of the app (authentication, journaling, chat, community, notifications).
  • To generate AI-assisted responses in the chat and to produce mood insights from your journal entries.
  • To protect the service against abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
  • To send you reminders and notifications that you have explicitly enabled.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your journal or chat content to train third-party advertising models.

3. How we share your information

We share limited information only with service providers that are necessary to operate Sakina:

  • Cloud infrastructure and databases that host our backend services.
  • Anthropic and/or other AI model providers that process the text of your chat messages to generate replies. These providers act as our processors and are contractually prohibited from using your content to train their general-purpose models beyond what is strictly required to deliver the response.
  • Email and SMS delivery providers used to send verification codes.
  • Apple Push Notification service (APNs) and Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push delivery.
  • Crash reporting providers for diagnostic data only.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law, to respond to valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Sakina, our users, or the public.

4. AI processing and safety

When you send a message to the AI assistant, the message text and a short window of recent conversation context are sent to our AI model provider to generate a reply. The reply is returned to you and stored in your chat history. Model providers may temporarily retain this content for safety review, abuse monitoring, and error investigation in accordance with their own policies.

AI-generated content may contain mistakes or inaccuracies. Do not rely on the AI assistant for medical, legal, financial, or emergency decisions.

5. Data retention

  • Account and content data are retained until you delete your account.
  • Login attempt records are retained for up to 12 months for security auditing, with any link to a deleted user removed.
  • Crash and diagnostic data are retained for up to 90 days.
  • Backups are retained for up to 35 days and are overwritten on a rolling basis.

6. Your choices and rights

You can exercise the following rights directly from inside the app:

  • Access and export: request a copy of your data from within the app.
  • Correction: edit your profile or journal entries at any time.
  • Deletion: open Settings → Privacy → Delete My Account to permanently erase your journal, chat history, community activity, and profile. This process is self-service and does not require contacting support. Apple Guideline 5.1.1(v) and Google Play’s User Data policy are satisfied by this flow.
  • Opt out of notifications: turn notifications off in the app or in your device settings.

Depending on where you live, you may also have rights under laws such as the EU/UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), Canada’s PIPEDA, or Singapore’s PDPA. To exercise those rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Security

All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS 1.2+). Passwords are stored as salted BCrypt hashes and are never transmitted or logged in plaintext. Access to production databases is restricted to a small number of authorized personnel and is gated behind multi-factor authentication. No system is perfectly secure; please use a unique, strong password and enable two-factor authentication where available.

8. Children’s privacy

Sakina is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. International users

Sakina is operated from the regions where our service providers host our infrastructure. By using the app you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in those regions, which may have different data protection laws than your own.

10. Changes to this policy

We will post any material changes on this page and update the “Last updated” date. If the changes are significant we will also notify you inside the app.

Questions, requests, or complaints: privacy@sakina.app.
